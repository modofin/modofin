About Modofin

At Modofin, we build bridges between Finland and China through trustworthy, two-way trade. We help Chinese customers access high-quality lifestyle products from Finland — from wellness goods to everyday essentials. Meanwhile, we assist Finnish businesses in sourcing mechanical products and components from China. With deep experience in cross-border logistics, product sourcing, and cultural communication, Modofin is your reliable partner in international commerce.
